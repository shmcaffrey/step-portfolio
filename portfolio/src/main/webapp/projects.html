2019-2020

21st Century Hammond Organ

With a team of two other Computer Science students, two Electrical engineering students, and a Mechanical engineering student, we've embarked on a mission to create a 21st century organ. Through The University of Michigan Arts Engine program and Multidisiplinary Design Program (MDP), I suggested a 21st century organ is an organ anyone can play, even if you don't know anything about music. We're currently in our mid-year break and will resume work in the fall.

2019

Euchre

In partnership with another CS student, we coded the Michigan card game Euchre using inheritance in C++. I sppecifically helped to make sure we met mini deadlines we'd set for ourselves along with partner coding. and took the lead on testing and version control

2018

Arduino: Space Invaders

In a group of 4 women coders, we recreated the classic 'Space Invaders' using an Arduino and screen. Players would use a potentiometer to move their space ship and a button to fire against invaders.

Arduino: Fall Down!

As an extention to the Space Invaders project, my team recreated the popular app 'Fall Down!' using an Arduino and potentiometer. Players would move a ball through platforms which scrolled up the screen at an increasing speed. I contributed to the code design, testing, version control, and finding a bug that gave us inconsistent results.
